## 2. Problem Statement: Invisible Barriers

### 2.1 AI Inequality: 21st Century Digital Colonialism

Just as the previous industrial revolution brought about inequality in physical labor, the AI revolution is creating **inequality in 'thinking and intelligence'**.

**Real-World Numbers:**

| Indicator | Current Status |
|:---|:---|
| AI Computational Resource Concentration | Top 1% of companies own over 90% of global GPUs |
| AI Service Costs | $20/month (Developed country youth: 4 cups of coffee / Global South youth: 10-30% of monthly living expenses) |
| AI Startup Investment | US and China account for over 80% of global investment |

**This is the true face of 21st-century digital colonialism.**

## 4. Solution: The Ethical Bridge

AlmaNEO does not define AI companies as enemies. Instead, we invite them to participate as **'Resource Providers'** in the ecosystem.

### 4.1 GAII Dashboard (The Moral Lever)

> *"Problems that are not measured cannot be solved."*

- **Function**: Ranks and publicly displays not only country-level AI inequality indices but also global AI companies' **'AI Inclusivity Scores'** in real-time.
- **Strategic Effect**: This metric acts as an **ESG standard**, creating **moral leverage** that incentivizes companies to donate or supply resources cheaply to the AlmaNEO ecosystem for image enhancement.
- **Current Status**: GAII data collection for 50 countries complete, Report v1.0 ready for publication

### 4.2 AlmaNEO AI Hub (Relay DePIN)

> *"Don't competeâ€”connect."*

Our DePIN focuses on **'exchange and distribution'** rather than 'production'.

**Exchange Mechanism (The Great Exchange):**

| AlmaNEO Provides | Big Tech Provides |
|:---|:---|
| **Diversity data** generated by users across 14 language regions | High-performance GPU cloud during idle hours |
| **Ethical feedback (RLHF)** - Key to solving AI bias | API access and credits |

**Redistribution:**
AI Hub prioritizes supplying acquired computing power to **'verified kind talent (Kindness SBT holders)'** at low cost (or free).

### 4.3 Kindness Protocol (Proof of Humanity)

> *"In the AI era, the scarcest resource is 'humanity'."*

- **Jeong-SBT (Soulbound Token)**: Permanently records users' good deeds, contributions, and data provision activities on the blockchain. A **'certificate of humanity'** that bots cannot imitate.
- **Ambassador SBT**: A 4-tier system automatically issued based on offline activities such as meetup attendance, hosting, and mentoring
- **Kindness Game**: Users naturally participate in AI training data labeling or feedback while enjoying games. This labor is recognized as 'mining' and rewarded with **ALMAN tokens**.
