## A5. OpenAI Researcher Access Program

**URL:** https://openai.com/form/researcher-access-program/
**Target:** $1,000 API credits (12 months)

### Researcher Name
Ruca Lee

### Affiliation
AlmaNEO Research Initiative

### Research Question
How does asymmetric AI adoption create measurable economic stratification across income levels, geographic regions, and linguistic communities — and what quantitative framework can make this stratification visible, trackable, and actionable for policy?

### Research Description
We are developing the Global AI Inequality Index (GAII) — the first composite metric quantifying AI access disparities across 50 countries (expanding to 100). GAII measures four dimensions: Access (40%), Affordability (30%), Language Support (20%), and Skills (10%). Our preliminary findings show AI adoption in the Global North (33.2%) is 3.5x higher than the Global South (9.5%), and AI subscription costs consume 25-28% of monthly income in the poorest countries.

### How will you use OpenAI products?
1. GPT-4o for analyzing AI policy documents across 100 countries in multiple languages
2. Processing and synthesizing multilingual survey data on AI access barriers
3. Generating comparative country-level AI inequality reports
4. Text embedding models for clustering AI policy approaches across regions
5. Evaluating language model performance disparities across our 14 supported languages (including Khmer, Swahili, Vietnamese) as part of GAII's Language Support indicator

### Research Area
Interdisciplinary research / Fairness & representation / Societal impact of AI

### Published Work / Live Platform
- GAII Dashboard (live): https://almaneo.org (Platform > GAII Dashboard)
- GAII Report v1.0 (downloadable PDF): https://almaneo.org (Platform > GAII Report)
