## Full Description (max 4,000 chars)
### English
AlmaChat connects kind people around the world through AI-powered real-time translation. Break language barriers and build genuine friendships across cultures.

KEY FEATURES:

Auto Translation (15 Languages)
Every message is automatically translated in real-time. Chat naturally in your language — Korean, English, Chinese, Japanese, Vietnamese, Thai, Indonesian, Arabic, Spanish, French, Portuguese, Hindi, German, Russian, and Turkish.

Global Community
Join channels, create group chats, and discover people from different cultures. AlmaChat is built for meaningful human connections, not just messaging.

Meetup System
Organize and join real-world meetups. Create meetup events, invite participants through group chat, and record meetup moments. Build friendships that go beyond the screen.

Kindness Score
Earn points for positive community activities — hosting meetups, helping new members, translating content, and more. Your Kindness Score reflects your contribution to building a warmer world.

Ambassador SBT
Achieve milestones and earn on-chain Ambassador badges. From "Kindness Friend" to "Kindness Guardian" — your kindness is permanently recorded.

Partner Network
Discover verified local businesses, cafes, and cultural spaces. Use exclusive vouchers from partner stores. Support kindness-friendly businesses in your area.

Secure Login
Sign in easily with Google, Apple, or other social accounts through Web3Auth. No complicated setup — just tap and chat.

AlmaChat is part of the AlmaNEO ecosystem, an AI democratization platform bridging technology and humanity through the Korean philosophy of Jeong (情) — deep emotional bonds between people.
